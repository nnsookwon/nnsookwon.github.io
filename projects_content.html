Bathroom Wars

An Android social media/game application. Claim territory by visiting various restroom locations, and conquer more land than your friends!

Click here to download from the Google Play Store!

Features:

  • Browse map to see nearby restroom locations and add new locations
  • Log restroom visit and "claim territory"
  • View personal map of conquered territories
  • "Battle" friends (connected via Facebook) and see map of both territories. Compare to see who's conquered more land. Buttons to toggle visibility of territory

Created using:

  • Android SDK
  • Java
  • Firebase, Google Maps, Google Services, and Facebook API

Flappy Pug

A twist on the original addictive game, made for Pug Lovers (like myself)!
